                          KEY TECHNOLOGIES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Backend:     Python, FastAPI, spaCy, Ollama, PyMuPDF, Tesseract
Database:    Neo4j 5.x (property graph)
Frontend:    React 18, Vite, Cytoscape.js
NLP:         spaCy (fr/en), llama3.1, sentence-transformers
Resolution:  rapidfuzz, embeddings, human-in-loop

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                           DATA FLOW EXAMPLE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Upload "Annual_Report_2023.pdf"
   ↓
2. Extract 50 pages → 120 chunks
   ↓
3. For each chunk:
   - spaCy finds: "Poulina Group", "Abdelwaheb Ben Ayed"
   - LLM extracts: FOUNDED relation with evidence
   ↓
4. Resolve duplicates:
   - "Poulina" + "PGH" → merge as "Poulina Group"
   ↓
5. Store in Neo4j:
   - Person node: "Abdelwaheb Ben Ayed"
   - Group node: "Poulina Group"
   - Relation: FOUNDED (confidence: 0.95, source: Annual_Report_2023.pdf)
   ↓
6. Query: "Who founded Poulina Group?"
   → Returns: "Abdelwaheb Ben Ayed" with evidence
